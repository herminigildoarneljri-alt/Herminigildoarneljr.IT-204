          Practical Applications of Nature's Reflection
Mindful Observation: Engaging our senses to fully experience 
the present moment,
such as watching a sunrise or feeling the wind, grounds us.
Nature Photography: The subjects we are drawn to in nature can reflect 
our unconscious,
inner thoughts and emotional state.
Metaphorical Thinking: Finding parallels between natural elements 
(e.g., rivers flowing, trees rooting) 
and our own lives helps us contextualize our problems and find solutions.
